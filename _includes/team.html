Meet Our Team

Dr. Annalee Nissenholtz

Founder & President

Before founding the Education Advisory Group, Dr. Nissenholtz worked as an independent college counselor, assisting over 1,000 families across the U.S., China, Canada, New Zealand, Myanmar, and India. She not only helped students gain admission to every top 200 U.S. college but also mentored and supervised over 60 counselors, developing innovative programs to enhance the services provided to families.

Dr. Nissenholtz’s exceptional contributions to college counseling have earned her widespread recognition. She has served as one of only 12 counselors on the U.S. News & World Report College Counselors Advisory Board and has been honored by several prestigious universities. Her additional leadership roles include:

  • University of North Carolina College Counselor Board
  • Washington University College Counselor Board
  • St. Louis University College Counselor Board
  • Counselor Trainer and Supervisor for College Bound, St. Louis.

"With over 35 years of experience, I have dedicated my career to helping students and their families navigate the ever-changing college admissions process.

Whether you’re a high-achieving student with a clear vision for your future or a student still exploring your interests, finding the right path after high school can feel overwhelming for both students and their families. The earlier you start planning, the better equipped you’ll be to make informed, confident decisions about your future.

At our practice, we provide world-class college counseling services to students across the globe. We understand the unique needs of every student and appreciate the diverse cultural perspectives of our international families. Our goal is to help you find, apply to, and get admitted to a school where you will thrive—academically, socially, and personally."

Beth McKenzie

EAG Team Member

Beth brings a wealth of experience and expertise to the Education Advisory Group team, having taught at Middlebury, where she worked with international students to elevate their language proficiency to meet the rigorous demands of graduate-level study. Her courses went beyond language instruction, equipping students with a deep understanding of both the formal and informal expectations of American higher education, as well as pedagogy and second language acquisition.

A proud Middlebury graduate with a master’s degree in Applied Linguistics/ESL, Beth is a recognized expert in communication and public speaking. She has developed a specialized interview training program to prepare students for conversations with admissions officers, ensuring they feel confident and make a lasting impression.

Beth’s global perspective is shaped by six years of teaching and mentoring students across China, Canada, Taiwan, and the United States. Her passion for building meaningful relationships with students shines through in her counseling approach, creating a supportive and engaging environment that helps students reach their full potential.

Beth excels in guiding students to discover their interests and identify extracurricular activities that are not only impactful but also deeply meaningful. She is especially skilled at working with younger students in grades 9 and 10, helping them lay a strong foundation for future academic and personal success. For juniors and rising seniors, Beth leverages her expertise in writing instruction to assist students in brainstorming, drafting, and refining compelling Personal Statements.

When Beth isn’t mentoring students, she’s likely tending to her garden, experimenting in the kitchen, or exploring hiking trails near her home in Germany. Her dynamic background, dedication to her students, and enthusiasm for education make her an invaluable member of the Education Advisory Group team.

Laura Luo

Intake Consultation Specialist

Since 2021, as a mother of two, Laura has been carving out new opportunities in the education field and thoroughly enjoying the journey. As an Intake Consultation Specialist, she not only communicates the company’s vision clearly to every parent but also focuses on guiding prospective families through the initial stages of the consultation process.

She carefully assesses the needs of students and their families, actively communicates with advisors, provides tailored recommendations, and ensures the smooth progression of services.

Laura is dedicated to fostering a trusting and collaborative environment. She is passionate about understanding each student’s individual goals and supporting advisors in helping every student achieve success.
