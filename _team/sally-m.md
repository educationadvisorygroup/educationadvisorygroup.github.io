**Sally is a highly accomplished education strategist with more than 25 years of proven success helping students stand out and secure their top-choice opportunities.** As a former university admissions reviewer and seasoned advisor, she has guided applicants through some of the most selective processes in higher education and the professional world.  

Sally spent years shaping future leaders at the prestigious Olin Business School at Washington University in St. Louis, where she developed and taught graduate-level coursework in managerial communication and contributed her expertise to graduate admissions decisions. Her unique perspective from “both sides of the table” means she knows exactly what admissions committees value — and how to position students for success. 

Throughout her career, Sally has empowered students from a wide range of academic interests, backgrounds, and learning styles to succeed. Her calm, personalized coaching approach builds confidence, elevates communication skills, and prepares students to present themselves with clarity, authenticity, and impact — whether through written personal statements, interviews, or professional video introductions. 

Sally is a recognized authority in communication skills development. She is known for her expertise in helping students create compelling resumes and she prepares all of our EAG students who are invited to interview with college admission officers at a highly selective university.

Her former students have gone on to highly competitive programs and prestigious careers at global corporations, Big Four accounting firms, and top investment institutions. Families trust Sally for her warmth, wisdom, and unwavering commitment to each student’s unique goals.

Sally is excited to partner with students and families at Education Advisory Group — helping them discover their strengths, unlock their potential, and achieve exceptional results in college admissions and beyond.
